Questions
Frequently asked

What's the difference between the two lists?

The buyer list includes active investors, 1031 exchange buyers, and institutional capital looking to acquire. The broker list includes net lease brokers and advisors who may have buyers or co-brokerage opportunities.

How long until my blast goes out?

Most blasts are reviewed and sent within 24–48 hours. You'll get a confirmation when it goes live.

Can I blast to both lists?

Yes — just submit two separate blasts, one for each list. Each is priced independently.

Do replies go to me or to you?

Directly to you. Your email is the reply-to address. Every lead and inquiry lands in your inbox instantly.

What property types qualify?

Any net lease property — single tenant, multi-tenant, ground lease, NNN, NN, modified gross, and more.

Can I send multiple properties?

Yes. Each property is a separate blast. Volume discounts available for 5+ properties.
